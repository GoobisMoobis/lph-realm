Los Pollos Hermanos Realm Projects: THE PBC

The PBC was a public bank built by Sandkins12 (Phoenix) in Realm 2 Season 2 of the Los Pollos Hermanos realm.

The PBC, built up of a simple square ground floor, had a not-so-secret underground area in which valuables were stored in vaults with different passwords.

It is unclear, but speculated that the PBC was owned by the much more powerful BNR due to the BNR ALSO being ran partly by Phoenix.

The PBC was built next to the BNR.


VAULTS

The vaults in the PBC's underground area were 3x3 piston doors. Each one had a different password.

HISTORY

The PBC was built about 1 week after the BNR started costruction.

The PBC was damaged by part of an attack by Nathan (runy8f)

During the end of the season 2 realm, everyone was given creative for fun, and tnt rained from the sky. the PBC no longer existed by that point.
