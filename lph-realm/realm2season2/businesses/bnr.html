Los Pollos Hermanos Realm Projects: THE BNR

Note:The BNR still exists today! Please visit it's website page, https://lph-realm.glitch.me/businesses/bnr.html

The BNR is the governing entity behind most of the projects in the second season of realm 2.

It was operated and owned by GoobisMoobis000 (Fender), the owner of both the realm and this website.

The BNR was constructed in the side of a mountain near a village.

Due to buisness at the BNR, all of the farms he had built, and everything else he had done, the BNR caused Fender to become the richest player in season 2, with a wealth of so many emerald blocks he could afford to give away stacks and stacks and easily recoup the losses.

The BNR was built up of a main area, with a zombie named Jeremy III Jr as a receptionist. Behind Jeremy was a secret pressure plate leading to the back of the BNR.

The back of the BNR had 2 things: a keycard reader, and special 3x3 piston door.

The 3x3 piston door was the entry point to the shared treasury, a giant chest area that anybody could donate to.

If a buisness dealing required resources from the treasury, it was used for that too.

If the correct keycard was put into the reader, a secret door would open up.

This door led down into the dungeon (potion farm of the BNR) and jail.

right under the dungeon, you'd recieve mining fatigue, to prevent prisoner escapes.

the jail no longer exists with new BNR buildings, but was used to punish people who commited crimes.


Roles

The BNR had many jobs to help with it's operations. This includes:

Job Common Nicknames Description
Representative Rep, R Representatives are the first point of contact for operations at the BNR. They act as a filter/summerizer. Most things people had questions about, a representative could answer or do. If they couldn't, the representative could set up a meeting between a superior and the client to get their needs sorted out.
Jail Guard JG Jail Guards were special employees who had access to the jail in the BNR's backroom basement. They made sure that even if a prisoner spent the grueling minutes it took to break a block with their fist + mining fatigue, that they'd still be captured in jail. Jail guards no longer exist.
Tresury Guard TG, T Treasury Guards guarded the treasury from theives who sought to steal from the BNR. They also were a first-responder for escaped prisoners.
Protector P Protectors were almost like a higher level of Treasury/Jail Guard. They fought ANY and ALL battles for the BNR. If the BNR were to get into a war, protectors were the people fighting the battles. As such, they were paid the most. They also helped with escaped prisoners or theives.
Resource Gatherer RG, R Resource Gatherers were anybody who mainly gathered things for the treasury and/or farms. Simple job with good pay.
